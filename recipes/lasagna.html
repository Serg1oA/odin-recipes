Code Lasagna

Description: Dive into coding culture with "Code Lasagna", a playful recipe that combines programming humor with culinary creativity.

Ingredients:

  • 9 lines of lasagna code (noodles)
  • 2 cups of algorithmic tomato sauce
  • 1 pound of binary beef or tofu tech-bits
  • 2 cups of debugged cheese blend
  • 1 cup of recursive ricotta
  • Syntax seasoning: garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your "Virtual Oven" to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brown binary beef or tofu tech-bits in a "Compiler" skillet.
  3. Mix "Recursive Ricotta" with syntax seasoning.
  4. Layer: Sauce, 3 lines of "Lasagna Code," half of beef/tofu, debugged cheese.
  5. Repeat: Sauce, 3 lines of code, "Recursive Ricotta."
  6. Layer again: Sauce, 3 lines of code, remaining beef/tofu, debugged cheese.
  7. Top: Sauce, 3 lines of code, and more debugged cheese.
  8. Bake with "Exception Handling Foil" for 25-30 minutes.
  9. Let your "Code Lasagna" rest, then serve.

Enjoy this coding-inspired culinary creation, packed with laughs and layers of flavor!
